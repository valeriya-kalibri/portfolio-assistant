# Career Timeline

Her most recent and current role is **Owner, AI Consultant & Engineer at Kalibri
Studios**, which she has led since January 2026. Interestingly, this is her second
time running Kalibri Studios — more on that below. In this role she builds and
deploys AI agents for customer-facing automation (knowledge retrieval, lead capture,
real-time conversational support) using LLM APIs, retrieval-augmented architectures,
and vector search. She develops real-time AI systems for live interaction contexts,
including voice-based customer engagement and in-call sales guidance, using
structured output pipelines and telephony integrations. She architects end-to-end
agentic workflows that orchestrate multi-step business processes with tool access,
external API calls, and structured data handoff, and she's built full SaaS AI
platforms — architecture, backend, and deployment — enabling automated analytics and
workflow tooling for non-technical operators. She leads every stage of client work
herself, from discovery through deployment. She started this chapter of Kalibri
Studios while still finishing out her role at HII, then left HII in March 2026 to
run it full-time.

Immediately before that, she was a **3D Technical Artist III at Mission Technologies,
HII** (Feb 2024 – March 2026), delivering production systems for Navy VR training
simulations under strict DoD technical specifications and real-time performance
constraints. She was promoted from contractor to full-time within a year, based on
the quality and reliability of her technical output — a defense contractor doesn't
extend that on goodwill.

Before HII, she was a **Senior 3D Artist at The DiSTI Corporation** (Jun 2021 – Feb
2024), developing and optimizing production assets for military VR simulation
pipelines across multiple concurrent programs, and serving as the technical point of
contact for asset standards and delivery readiness.

Before that, she ran the *first* iteration of **Kalibri Studios**, as Lead 3D
Generalist / Technical Artist (Jun 2018 – Jun 2021) — a commercial 3D visualization
studio where she owned scoping, timelines, quality standards, and client
communication across multiple simultaneous programs, and managed external
contributors. So the AI-focused Kalibri Studios she runs today isn't her first time
building and operating her own company — it's her second.

Her earliest production role was as a **CG Generalist at MediaLab 3D Solutions /
Stone Falcon Game Studio** (2016 – 2018), creating 3D models, shaders, and texture
maps, and producing photo-realistic renderings across multiple simultaneous projects.
