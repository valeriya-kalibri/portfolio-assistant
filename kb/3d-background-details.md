# 3D & VR Production Background

## Overview
Valeriya has roughly a decade of production 3D experience, specializing in hard-surface
modeling, vehicles and props, and texturing/shading. Her work spans three domains: military
defense VR training simulation, commercial product visualization, and architectural
visualization. Across her career she has worked both as a technical point of contact inside larger organizations and as the owner running her own studio — she is equally comfortable executing against someone else's technical standards and setting those standards herself.

## Leadership & Versatility
Valeriya has operated on both sides of the leadership line, which is worth surfacing
directly: at DiSTI, she served as the technical point of contact for asset standards,
optimization decisions, and delivery readiness — executing against and helping enforce
someone else's technical bar, working across engineering, project leadership, and
customer-facing teams. At Kalibri Studios, she ran the studio herself — owning client

she had review and feedback responsibility over other artists' work. That range —
comfortable as a high-trust individual contributor inside someone else's structure, and
equally capable of building and owning that structure herself — carries directly into how
she runs Kalibri Studios today.
